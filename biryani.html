Dish name: Chicken Dum Biryani

Price: ₹320

About it:
Chicken Dum Biryani is a rich and aromatic rice dish made by layering marinated chicken with partially cooked basmati rice, then slow-cooking (dum) it all together.
Flavored with saffron, fried onions, and whole spices like cardamom and cloves, it has a royal touch and deep flavor.
Each bite offers a perfect mix of tender chicken and fragrant rice, making it one of the most loved biryanis in India.

Dish name: Shrimp Dum Biryani

Price: ₹350

About it: Shrimp Dum Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic seafood twist on the traditional biryani.
Juicy shrimp are marinated in spices and layered with fragrant basmati rice, herbs, and fried onions, then slow-cooked using the dum method to seal in all the flavors.
The result is a rich, mildly spicy dish with the perfect blend of coastal and Mughlai flavors, usually enjoyed with raita or a boiled egg on the side.

Dish name: Mutton biryani

Price: ₹500

About it: Mutton Biryani is a classic and hearty dish made with tender, marinated mutton pieces layered with aromatic basmati rice, saffron, and whole spices like bay leaf, cinnamon, and cloves.
Slow-cooked using the dum method, it allows all the flavors to blend beautifully.
Known for its rich taste and melt-in-your-mouth meat.

Dish name: Chicken fry biryani

Price: ₹310

About it: Chicken Fry Biryani is a delicious fusion where crispy, spiced fried chicken is combined with fragrant biryani rice.
Unlike traditional biryanis, the chicken is cooked separately and then mixed with the rice, giving it a bold and flavorful punch.
The biryani is often garnished with fried onions and fresh herbs, offering a satisfying mix of textures and tastes—perfect for spice lovers.
