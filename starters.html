Dish name: Maharashtrian Chicken Sukka

Price: ₹250

About it:
Maharashtrian Chicken Sukka is a spicy and flavorful dry chicken dish made with freshly ground coconut, aromatic spices, and a hint of tanginess.
It’s a traditional recipe from the coastal regions of Maharashtra, often enjoyed with bhakri or rice.
The word "sukka" means dry, and the dish gets its rich taste from the slow cooking of chicken with roasted spices and coconut.

Dish name: Mexican Dry Rub Chicken

Price: ₹280

About it: Mexican Dry Rub Chicken is a bold and zesty dish made by coating chicken with a mix of Mexican spices like paprika, cumin, chili powder, garlic, and oregano.
It’s typically grilled or baked until juicy and tender, with a crispy, flavorful outer crust.
This dish brings out smoky and spicy flavors and is often served with salsa, tortillas, or salad.

Dish name: Spicy Korean Chicken

Price: ₹300

About it: Spicy Korean Chicken is a fiery and flavorful dish made with tender chicken pieces coated in a rich, sticky sauce of gochujang (Korean chili paste), garlic, soy sauce, and a touch of sweetness.
It’s usually grilled or pan-fried, giving it a slightly crispy texture.
This dish is popular for its perfect balance of heat, sweetness, and umami, often served with rice or lettuce wraps.

Dish name: Thai Sticky Chicken

Price: ₹290

About it: Thai Sticky Chicken is a deliciously glazed dish featuring chicken cooked in a sweet, savory, and slightly spicy sauce made from soy sauce, fish sauce, garlic, and brown sugar.
As it cooks, the sauce thickens and clings to the chicken, giving it a glossy, sticky finish.
It's bursting with Thai flavors and is typically served with jasmine rice or sticky rice, making it a comforting and satisfying meal.
